About us

Quality education is one of FNs sustainable development goals and is very important to secure the future and knowledge of children that live in less developed countries. The countries on the south of Sahara have the highest absence rates in schools. According to UIS and UNESCO are approximately 60% of children between 12-17 years old out of school - this corresponds to almost 100 million children, and 1/3 of the children in the whole world. Poverty and the quality of the schools are some of the main reasons why it is so common not to go to school. We have come up with a solution, which we belive is the most effective way of working towards this goal.
